About Us

Huron Investment Club (HIC) is Huron at Western University's largest finance community on campus with membership over 150 students. Our primary mandate is to equipt Huron students with the knowledge and practices needed to succeed as value investors. We welcome every students to participate including ones from Western University and affiliate colleges.

Portfolio Management

Led by student portfolio managers, HIC will hold weekly meetings for each portfolio team to cover buy/sell discussions, research troubleshoots, and pitches presented by analysts since the 22/23 school year.

Membership Benefits

Educational Resources

Members are encouraged to learn about variety of financial fundamentals from pdf files, book recommendations, podcasts, and many more (including intrinsic valuation modelling). Click the Github icon at the footer to access our free resource package.

Investment Portfolios

Enhance your analytical tools, financial learning curve, and public speaking skills through 5 portfolio groups tailored to your industry of interest.

Seminars / Networking Events

From guest speaker events, educational seminars, workshops, company information sessions, and socials, HIC engage our members with a variety of activities.

Mentorship

HIC is planning a mentorship program starting from the 22/23 school year. Upper-year mentors share their knowledge and experience for their mentees and other HIC members to learn from.
